HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commissioning artwork and the creative process

How long does a commission take?
Typically, a portrait takes 2-4 weeks depending on size and complexity. Oil paintings may require additional drying time. Rush orders can be accommodated with prior discussion.
What do I need to provide?
High-quality reference photos are essential. For best results, provide clear, well-lit images from multiple angles. We'll discuss your vision, preferred size, and medium during consultation.
What are your rates?
Pricing varies based on size, medium, complexity, and timeline. A4 pencil portraits start from competitive rates, while larger oil paintings are quoted individually. Contact me for a personalized quote.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! I can ship completed artworks internationally with secure packaging. Local delivery is available within Algeria. Shipping costs and methods will be discussed before finalizing your order.
Can I see progress updates?
Absolutely! I provide regular progress photos throughout the creation process. You'll have opportunities to request adjustments during key stages to ensure the final piece matches your vision perfectly.
